about

My name is Lucy Whalley and I am a PhD student in the Walsh Materials Design group at Imperial College London. My research is based around modelling the electronic structure of materials that are used for solar cells. Funding for my research is through the Centre for Doctoral Training in New and Sustainable Photovoltaics. I am very happy to have a PhD project which combines my training in solid state physics, enjoyment of programming and interest in renewable energy.

Before my PhD I trained as a mathematics teacher, with specialism in Post-Compulsory Education and Training. I have worked as a teacher in a variety of settings - prisons, primary schools and cafes. Software Carpentry.
